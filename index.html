About the Enron Dataset

The Enron corpus is a massive database of over 500,000 emails generated by 158 employees of the Enron Corporation. It became public during the legal investigation and has since become a valuable resource for research in fields like:

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Social Network Analysis
  • Fraud Detection Research
  • Organizational Behavior Studies
